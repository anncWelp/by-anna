VISION-MISSION STATEMENT

La Concepcion College envisions itself as one of the leading educational institutions that serves as a catalyst for change toward the holistic development of the community, the country, and the ASEAN region.

Inspired by the motherly care and ideals of Mary Immaculate Conception, and guided by the core values that define a true LCCian, LCC dedicates itself to inspiring students and stakeholders to reach their fullest potential by cultivating minds, capturing hearts, and changing lives to become better individuals in society.
